About Me

I'm Alex, a designer based in San Francisco. My journey began when I tried to customize my Myspace profile and accidentally discovered I loved code. Since then, I've been obsessively simplifying complex interfaces.

I believe that good design is invisible. It should work so smoothly that the user doesn't even notice it's there. When I'm not pushing pixels in Figma, you can find me brewing pour-over coffee, hiking the Pacific Coast trails, or attempting to keep my houseplants alive.
